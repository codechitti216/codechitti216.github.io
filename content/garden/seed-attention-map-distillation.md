Standard distillation operates at three levels: latent space alignment, prediction matching, and ground-truth supervision. But what if you also forced the student to attend to the same regions as the teacher — via a loss on the attention maps or Grad-CAM activations?

TinyBERT and MiniLM do this in NLP. Nobody has done it for BEV perception in autonomous driving.
